# Romanesque
#romanesque
## Religion
- More importance placed in the holy, representing figures of the church with conservative concepts of dignity
- in context of roman pantheism and art
## Art
- Simple, Natural, Static, Frontal, Cheaper materials, Authority and Symbolism
- Heavily inspired by roman art and stoicism

# Gothic
#gothic 
## Religion
- Humanizing figures of the church, interpreting stories as literal visual representations
- Individualizing the interpretations of the visuals to connect with the intended viewers
- translating the ideals of heaven to the reality of Earth
- Stories told in panels or compressed narratives of symbolic moments
## Art
- Complex, Luxurious, Dynamic, Intense Pigments/Metallics, Literal interpretations of figures
- Context changing because of the changing times
- Pulpits were becoming the fashion and necessary for the structure of the chapels
- Still inspired by roman art, but with more movement and naturalism
