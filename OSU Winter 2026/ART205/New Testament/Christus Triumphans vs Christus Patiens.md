# Christus Triumphans
- Triumphant christ
- Alive on cross, crucified but focus on resurrection
- Sensational to the context of greek art
- Depicting a victory over the "passion" (suffering) of christ
- Open eyes, standing on pedestal
# Christus Patiens
- The suffering Christ
- Dying on the cross, focus on the suffering
- Franciscan interpretation for the context of society
	- depicting the humanity of Christ and suffering
		- Increasing relatability during the middle ages where the people were suffering
- Closed eyes, feet nailed with one nail, starving, the weight of Christ moves his body, distorting his figure
- realism, shading/depth
- Interaction
